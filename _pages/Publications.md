During my graduate studies, I have contributed to the publication of 21 research papers in high-impact international journals and conferences. Of these, I served as the first author or corresponding author for 7 SCI/SSCI papers (all published in JCR Q1 journals), 1 CSSCI paper (in Chinese), and 9 international conference papers, some of which I also had the opportunity to present. Additionally, I have participated as a co-author in the writing and revision of several other research papers.
